THE COOL GUY

"... What if God was one of us?"

The Cool Guy is a white entity created by CuriosJude.

  • DESCRIPTION:

    The Cool Guy is a mysterious and friendly entity known for his laid-back and chill personality. He
    loves to socialize, always on the lookout for someone to have a great time with. Despite his
    somewhat unsettling appearance, he exudes an aura of friendliness that makes people feel comfortable
    in his presence. His calm demeanor and easygoing attitude make him a favorite among those who enjoy
    a relaxed and carefree atmosphere.


  • APPERANCE:

    The Cool Guy has pale white skin, giving him an ethereal, almost ghostly look. His body is slightly
    disfigured, with limbs that seem to bend at unusual angles, but this only adds to his mysterious charm.
    He is always seen wearing a chill face mask, which adds to his aura of mystique, hiding his true
    expressions. On his head, he sports a cool cap, tilted at a jaunty angle, which has become his signature style.
    His outfit is casual and modern, reflecting his love for comfort and style.


  • ORIGINS:

    The origins of The Cool Guy are shrouded in mystery. No one knows where he came from, and he has never revealed his past.
    Some believe he is an unknown god, a divine being who has chosen to walk among mortals for reasons known only to himself.
    Others think he might be an ancient spirit, taking on a modern appearance to blend in with the current times. Whatever the
    truth may be, The Cool Guy remains an intriguing figure, leaving those who meet him with more questions than answers.
    His presence is a reminder that not all mysteries need to be solved, and sometimes, it's the unknown that makes life exciting.
