This page offers an exploration of the fascinating world of Filipino folklore, where you'll discover a wide range of mythical beings, spirits, and legendary creatures that have shaped the cultural imagination of the Philippines. Each creature has its own unique story, characteristics, and significance, reflecting the diverse beliefs and traditions of various ethnic groups across the archipelago. Whether you're a curious learner or a mythology enthusiast, this list provides a comprehensive guide to the rich tapestry of mythological entities that have been passed down through generations.
